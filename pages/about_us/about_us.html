About the Podcast

What are your top five Disney movies? What are the top five cities you’ve traveled to? What are your top five favorite things about Chicago? What are your top five karaoke songs? Jack and Cate invite two special guests on each episode to have conversations full of insight, attitude and passion where everyone must reveal their top fives, their finest fives! Join us every other week to hear a variety of opinions, perspectives and thoughts on topics ranging from movies, music, muffins and anything in between. Don’t forget about Debroah! Our deeply trusted and beloved internet friend who so effortlessly scans the web for data and evidence on what the ultimate top five list truly is.

Jack Comella

Jack is a senior at Loyola University Chicago studying Film & Digital Media. He enjoys spending his free time editing videos, photography, traveling, and listening to music. Jack loves hearing from others during conversations and podcasts and is going to bring that passion for listening to the Finest Fives Podcast!

Cate Plunkett

Cate is a senior at Loyola University Chicago studying journalism and marketing. She loves reading, spending time outside and being with her amazing friends. Cate is inspired by others’ opinions and creativity and she looks forward to each conversation had on The Finest Fives!
